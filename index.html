When it's hard — DBT

skills

Evidence-based distress-tolerance tools, phrased gently. Finishing one logs to your day on this device only.

TIPP

Temperature, Intense movement, Paced breathing, Paired muscle relaxation — a four-stage flow when the body is loud.

STOP

Stop, Take a breath, Observe, Proceed — a pause before acting on an urge.

Radical acceptance

Three short prompts: what is, what can't change, one small move — not agreement, just reality with kindness.

Reframe (thought record)

A five-step CBT-style path when a thought keeps looping — related but separate from DBT flows above.

Not emergency care. If you might hurt yourself, call or text 988 in the US, or your local crisis line.
